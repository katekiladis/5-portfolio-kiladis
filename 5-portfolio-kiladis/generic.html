My name is Kate Kiladis

Hi, my name is Kate Kiladis I am 17 years old and a Senior at Andover High School. My passions are Film Editing and Directing. I would like to major in Cinematography and/or Film and Video Production.

When I was 8 years old I fell in love with the art of movie making. I would write scripts and make my friends act then I would film them. It all started with iMovie, a video editing software for apple products, I started editing on my iPad Mini. I then moved from there onto Final Cut Pro and After Effects. I have taught numerous film classes to younger kids and teens about how to write a proper screenplay, how to shoot different shots on a camera, directing, and editing. In San Diego I attented a film production school in which I taught how to create your own short film.
