About Me

About
Me

My name is Kate Kiladis I am 18 years old, and a Senior at Andover High School. I am currently the president of my high school’s Film and Video Production Club. I'm passionate about expressing my creativity. My strengths are in are directing, editing, & cineamatography

When I was 9 years old, I fell in love with the art of creating content. I would write scripts, shoot clips, and ask my friends to appear in my videos. I began by shooting original footage and editing it with iMovie on my iPad. Before I knew it, I advanced to editing on Final Cut Pro, Premiere, and After Effects. Today I have over 10 videos on YouTube with over five million views. My original trailers and video recaps of the Netflix hit show “Stranger Things” have become viral hits generating over four million views. One of my greatest joys is sharing my filmmaking passion with others. In the summer of 2017, I traveled from my hometown of Andover, MA to enroll in a summer semester of Media Arts Center in San Diego, where I studied film production. My curriculum included writing, directing, producing, and editing. Shortly after I finished the program, my professors asked me to extend my stay and help teach production to younger students. I taught numerous film classes to children between the ages of seven to twelve. Our curriculum included, screenplay writing, videography, directing, and editing. Whether it's creating viral videos for the internet, producing projects at school, or teaching young people, I deeply enjoy sharing my passion with others. Being creative brings me joy every day and I look forward to growing and preparing myself for a lifetime of future creative endeavors.
